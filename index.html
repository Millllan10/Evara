About Us

Redefining Modern Elegance,
One Home at a Time

Designed for comfort and contemporary living, Evara Virar West delivers excellent ventilation, natural light, and absolute privacy in every residence — elevated by podium and rooftop amenities including landscaped gardens, fitness zones, and vibrant recreation spaces.

Located close to schools, hospitals, shopping hubs, and Western Railway connectivity, Evara offers the perfect balance of urban convenience and peaceful living — making it an ideal choice for homebuyers and investors alike.

FAQ

Your Questions,
Elegantly Answered

What makes your properties truly luxury?

+
Evara Virar West offers thoughtfully designed 2, 3 & 4 BHK Sky Deck Residences with spacious layouts, larger carpet areas, modern architecture, premium finishes, and over 40 curated amenities across podium and rooftop levels.

What locations do you currently develop in?

+
Evara Virar West is located at Global City, opposite McDonald's, Dongarpada, Virar West — a premier residential destination offering excellent connectivity to Mumbai and beyond via the Western Railway line.

Are your properties customizable to my preferences?

+
Our homes are designed with thoughtful layouts and premium materials. Our sales team can walk you through available configuration options and assist with personalising your home to suit your lifestyle needs.

Can I schedule a sample flat visit?

+
Yes, absolutely. You can call us at +91 9922 451 451 or fill the contact form to book a guided site visit at a time convenient to you. Our team will be happy to host you.

Who is the developer of Evara Virar West?

+
Evara Virar West is a flagship project by Poonam Developers in association with Neminath Group — both trusted names with decades of experience delivering landmark residential developments across the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.

What kind of after-sales services do you provide?

+
We offer comprehensive post-possession support including dedicated customer care, homeowner onboarding, and assistance with documentation. Our relationship with homeowners extends well beyond the point of handover.
